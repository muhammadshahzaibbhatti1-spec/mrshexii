About

mrshexii is a sanctuary for those who feel too deeply — a space where Urdu and English poetry breathe together, where classical masters meet modern voices.

یہ جگہ ان لوگوں کے لیے ہے جو الفاظ میں گھر ڈھونڈتے ہیں

Here you will find the complete ghazals of Ghalib, the revolutionary love of Faiz, the devastating wit of Jaun Elia — and between the verses, the raw confessions of a life lived through poetry.

Every poet has their own page. Every heartbreak has its blog post. Welcome home.

Life Confessions Blog

Not all wounds are made of words. Some are made of silences, of almost-said things, of 3am thoughts that never found a mouth. This blog is that mouth.
